Who we are


Welcome to 404 Cafe. Our space is for those who want to enjoy a warm drink of coffe, cold drink of coffee, or a strong drink of coffee.


We build this space so programmers could enjoy a nice drink and do some coding in a nice an relaxing environment. Come in to have a meeting, come in to catch up with friends, or just hang out and drinks some yummy drinks.


Our coffes are source from organic farms around the world and we have a rotating list of local beans as well.
